Our Mission and Goals

Our project is unique because it solves the problem of young adults feeling like they don't have a chance of getting college education and/or jobs because they didn’t go to college. This website should be used if you need a job, some housing opportunities, if you didn’t finish school, and/or want to finish. We believe in young adults deserve the chance to get somewhere even if they're not the perfect, straight A+ student. We want to give young adults th e chance to be able to have opportunities to finish school, to get a job, and or have housing opportunities.
